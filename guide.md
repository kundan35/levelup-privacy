# Vela Family — User Guide

**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** March 2026

---

## Getting Started

### Step 1 — Create a Parent Account

1. Download **Vela Family** from the Google Play Store on your phone.
2. Open the app and tap **Register**.
3. Enter your email address and create a password.
4. Verify your email (check your inbox for a confirmation link).
5. You are now logged in as a parent.

---

### Step 2 — Set Up Your Child's Device

1. Install **Vela Family** on your child's Android phone.
2. On the child's device, tap **"Using as a Kid?"** on the login screen.
3. Enter your **parent email address** — the app will find your family account.
4. Choose a **4-digit PIN** your child will use to log in.
5. Grant the required permissions (see below).

---

## Required Permissions on the Child's Device

These three permissions are required for the app to work:

| Permission | What It Does |
|------------|-------------|
| **Location** | Shows your child's real-time position and sends safe-zone alerts |
| **Usage Access** | Tracks which apps are open so time limits and blocks can be enforced |
| **Overlay (Draw over apps)** | Displays the "App Restricted" screen when a blocked app is opened |

### How to Grant Each Permission

**Location (Always Allow)**
1. Open Android **Settings → Apps → Vela → Permissions → Location**
2. Select **"Allow all the time"**

**Usage Access**
1. Open Android **Settings → Apps → Special app access → Usage access**
2. Find **Vela** and toggle it **On**

**Overlay**
1. Open Android **Settings → Apps → Special app access → Display over other apps**
2. Find **Vela** and toggle it **On**

---

## Recommended Permissions

These add extra monitoring features but are not required:

| Permission | Feature It Unlocks |
|------------|-------------------|
| **Notification Access** | WhatsApp & social notification previews |
| **Accessibility Service** | YouTube video & search history |
| **Call & SMS** | Call logs and SMS sender monitoring |
| **Battery Optimization (Ignore)** | Keeps monitoring running reliably on all devices |

## Parent Dashboard

### Adding a Child

1. Open the **Dashboard** tab.
2. Tap **+ Add Child**.
3. Follow the on-screen instructions to pair with your child's device.

### Viewing Activity

- **Dashboard** — Overview of all your children, recent alerts, and quick stats.
- **Alert Inbox** — Auto-generated alerts for flagged activity.
- **Activity Feed** — Full timeline of your child's device usage.

### Managing Apps

1. Tap a child's card on the Dashboard.
2. Select **App Controls**.
3. Toggle any app **On** (allowed) or **Off** (blocked).
4. To set a daily time limit, tap an app and enter the limit in minutes.

### Screen Time Limits

1. Open **Child Detail → Screen Time**.
2. Set a total daily limit or per-app limits.
3. Limits reset at midnight every day.

### Bedtime Lock

1. Open **Child Detail → Bedtime**.
2. Set a **Sleep Time** and **Wake Time**.
3. During the sleep window, all non-essential apps are locked.

### Exam Mode

1. Open **Child Detail → Exam Mode**.
2. Enable Exam Mode and set the end date.
3. Only study-category apps remain accessible.

---

## Approving App Requests

When your child tries to open a blocked app, they can send you an unblock request:

1. A notification will appear on your device.
2. Open **App Requests** from the Dashboard or Settings.
3. Tap **Approve** to allow the app or **Deny** to keep it blocked.

## Rewards & Gamification

### How It Works

- Your child earns **XP** by completing tasks and good habits.
- XP fills their level bar — higher levels unlock more app access.
- **Coins** are awarded alongside XP and can be spent in the **Reward Shop**.

### Creating Tasks

1. Open **Child Detail → Quests / Tasks**.
2. Tap **+ Add Task**.
3. Set a name, coin reward, and due date.

### Reward Shop

1. Open **Child Detail → Coin Shop**.
2. Add prizes your child can redeem (e.g. "30 minutes extra screen time", "Choose dinner").
3. When a child redeems a reward, you receive a notification to approve it.

### Sending Praise

Open any child's detail screen and tap **Send Praise** to instantly award bonus coins.

## Location & Safe Zones

### Viewing Live Location

1. Open **Child Detail → Location**.
2. The map shows your child's current position, updated in real-time.

### Setting Up a Geofence

1. On the location map, tap **+ Add Safe Zone**.
2. Draw a circle around the safe area (e.g. home, school).
3. You'll receive an alert whenever your child enters or leaves the zone.

## Notification Monitoring

Vela reads **notification previews** from WhatsApp and other social apps. It does not access full message history — only the notification text Android makes available.

Flagged messages from unknown contacts appear in the **Alert Inbox**.

---

## Family Mission

Create a shared goal your whole family works on together:

1. Open **Family Mission** from the Dashboard.
2. Create a mission with a target (e.g. "Read 30 min a day for a week").
3. All family members can mark progress — completing it rewards everyone.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can my child uninstall the app?**
No. Vela activates Device Admin on the child's device, which prevents uninstallation without your PIN.

**Q: Why is location not updating?**
Ensure "Always" location permission is granted: Settings → Apps → Vela → Permissions → Location → Allow all the time.

**Q: The app blocker isn't working — what should I do?**
Check that Usage Access permission is granted: Settings → Apps → Special app access → Usage access → Vela → On.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
Google Play → your profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Vela Family → Cancel.

**Q: How does social monitoring work?**
Vela reads incoming notification previews on the child's device. It does not read full message history.
